Manual Dial
Placing a call through this menu
Phone numbers can be typed in this menu by visiting the Dialpad tab. Pressing any key on your keyboard will automatically begin typing into the dial pad.
When you're ready to dial press enter to place your call.
Placing a call with a keyboard shortcut
In the Options tab you can set a keyboard shortcut to manually dial a number.
