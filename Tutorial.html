Usage:


Once the page has finished loading. Simply move your cursor over any phone numbers on the page.
Once the CC tooltip has appeared, clicking it will initiate your call.

Dialing a custom number:

You may also use Click2Call's tooltip to dial custom numbers.
Clicking on the edit button on the right side of the tooltip brings up a text box.
Simply enter the number you would like to dial and press the call button to place your call.
